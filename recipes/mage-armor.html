Potion of Mage Armor

Mage Armor has stood between adventurers and their would be demise on countless adventures. It can stop claws fangs arrows and more. But preparing mage armor each and every day is such a drag and wastes time that could be used to prep spells that will send your enemies into slumber leaving them at your mercy. Nyahahahah!

And that's where this potion comes in, just simply chug it and those stupid kobolds attacks will bounce off in futility.

ingrediants

  • 2 adamant seeds
  • 1/4 cup scales(dragon scales are best but any will do)
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 3 square inches of honey comb
  • a chunk of bark from and oak tree

brewing directions

  1. with a knife whittle the tree bark into wood shavings
  2. chop up the honeycomb into small pieces
  3. bring 1/2 a cup of water to a boil in the kettle
  4. pour in the scales and salt and stir 7 times counter clockwise
  5. add the oak bark and honey comb and stir thrice clockwise
  6. leave to boil until it reaches a syrupy considtency
  7. add in 2 cups of water then bring back to boil
  8. put in addamant seeds and wait until they burst
  9. pour potion into bottle
  10. deflect arrows with your bare hands
